{{ selectedGoal === 'weightGain' ? 'Weight Gain' : 'Weight Loss' }} Nutrition Plan:

Beginner Level (Weight Gain):

Week 1-4:

Calories: Aim for a daily surplus of 250-500 calories.

Protein: Consume 1.2-1.5 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight.

Carbohydrates: Make carbs about 45-55% of your daily calories.

Fats: Keep fats at about 20-30% of your daily calories.

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with banana and almonds.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and mixed vegetables.
  • Snack: A handful of mixed nuts.
  • Dinner: Salmon with sweet potato and broccoli.

Intermediate Level (Weight Gain):

Week 5-8:

Calories: Gradually increase the daily surplus to 500-750 calories.

Fine-tune macronutrient ratios based on your preferences and body's response.

Consider pre- and post-workout nutrition for muscle recovery.

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with berries.
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with a side salad.
  • Snack: Hummus with carrot and cucumber sticks.
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with brown rice and mixed vegetables.

Advanced Level (Weight Gain):

Week 9 and beyond:

Continue increasing calories as needed for your goals.

Monitor your progress closely and make adjustments.

Consider nutrient timing for optimal performance.

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Protein smoothie with spinach, protein powder, and almond butter.
  • Snack: A protein bar and a piece of fruit.
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with grilled shrimp and avocado.
  • Snack: Low-fat Greek yogurt with walnuts.
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with quinoa and roasted Brussels sprouts.

Beginner Level (Weight Loss):

Week 1-4:

Calories: Aim for a daily deficit of 300-500 calories.

Protein: Consume 1.2-1.5 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight.

Carbohydrates: Make carbs about 45-50% of your daily calories.

Fats: Keep fats at about 20-30% of your daily calories.

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries and a drizzle of honey.
  • Snack: Celery sticks with peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with a vinaigrette dressing.
  • Snack: Sliced bell peppers with hummus.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed asparagus.

Intermediate Level (Weight Loss):

Week 5-8:

Calories: Gradually decrease the daily deficit as you approach your goal weight.

Focus on portion control and mindful eating.

Consider intermittent fasting or time-restricted eating, if appropriate.

Sample Meal Plan:

  • Breakfast: Veggie omelet with a side of mixed berries.
  • Snack: Almonds and a small apple.
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado lettuce wraps.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks.
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with cauliflower rice and broccoli.

Advanced Level (Weight Loss):

Week 9 and beyond:

Continue monitoring calorie intake and adjust as needed.
